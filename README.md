# BloodLust
A blood/gore mod for the game Operation Harsh Doorstop.

## Integrating BloodLust into your own mod/pack/gamemode
- Copy the contents of BloodLust's `Core/BloodLust` folder into your mod's `Core` folder (or whatever you may have renamed your `Game Mode` folder to in your mod config.
- Include the ruleset `BP_HDRuleset_BloodLust` as part of your gamemode.
